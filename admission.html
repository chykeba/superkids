Admission

Application forms are available for sale in the school. Alternatively, forms may also be purchased, and printed from our website. Forms are to be completed and submitted to the school. An interview is scheduled for candidates in the pre-school while Pupils enrolling for year 1-6 will complete a computer-based assessment in reading and math, followed by an oral interview. School administration will use the data to determine the best setting for the Pupil.

Acceptance of admission must be communicated through payment of fees in designated banks and proof of payment submitted to the school’s office.

Furthermore, Pupils of SKS are assessed on a regular basis throughout the school year. Our teaching staff collect data on each Pupil to successfully meet the academic needs of every individual in all subjects. This data is monitored regularly to provide appropriate instruction and support to all Pupils.

School Fees

School Fees are due on or before the first day of school. They may be paid annually or termly.  However, the management reserves the right to review school fees as necessary in line with changes in costs associated with providing standard education. Due to limited slots for admission, early payment of fees before resumption will guarantee a spot for your child.

Note: Fees paid are non-refundable.
